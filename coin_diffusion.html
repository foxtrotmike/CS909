1D Diffusion Demo

Heads are values near +0.75 and tails are values near -0.75, both with a little Gaussian variation. The forward process gradually adds noise. The reverse model learns to predict that noise so it can denoise Gaussian noise back into the target distribution. In this version, the generated distribution is refreshed live during training so you can see it evolve.
